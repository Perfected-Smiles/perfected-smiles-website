Comprehensive Dental Care for Every Smile

From preventive care to advanced cosmetic treatments — we provide complete dental services under one roof.

Dental Fillings

Dental fillings are used to repair damaged teeth caused by cavities and help restore strength and function while protecting the surrounding tooth structure.

Tooth Extraction

Tooth extraction may be recommended when a tooth cannot be restored, and removing it can help protect surrounding teeth and oral health.

Dentures

Dentures provide a removable option for replacing missing teeth and improving the appearance and function of your smile.

Dental Implants

Dental implants provide a stable solution for replacing missing teeth by supporting crowns or dentures designed to restore function and appearance.

Invisalign

Invisalign treatment uses clear aligners that gradually move teeth into improved alignment for a more balanced smile.

Crowns & Bridges

Dental crowns and bridges help repair damaged teeth or replace missing teeth while supporting overall dental function.

Snap-In Dentures

Snap-in dentures combine dental implants with removable dentures to provide improved stability and comfort.

Veneers

Dental veneers are thin restorations that improve the appearance of teeth by enhancing shape, alignment, and color.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes, our dental office welcomes new patients who are searching for a dentist in Dearborn Heights. Our team works to make the process simple and convenient by helping patients schedule an appointment and complete the necessary paperwork. During your first visit, we will review your dental history and evaluate your oral health. X-rays may be taken if needed to better understand the condition of your teeth. Our goal is to help patients feel comfortable and informed during every step of their dental care.

Is your office accessible to patients with disabilities?

Our dental office works to provide a welcoming environment where patients can feel comfortable during their visit. We strive to accommodate patients who may require accessibility assistance when visiting our practice. If you have any specific needs or requests, our front desk staff will be happy to help coordinate your appointment. We encourage patients to contact our office ahead of time so we can provide the best possible experience. Supporting patient comfort and accessibility is an important part of our dental care philosophy.

What is your cancellation or rescheduling policy?

We understand that schedules can change and patients may need to reschedule their appointment. If you need to cancel or adjust your appointment time, we ask that you contact our dental office as soon as possible. Providing advance notice allows us to offer the appointment time to other patients who may need dental treatment. Our front desk staff will help you find another available appointment that fits your schedule. We appreciate your cooperation in helping our practice serve patients efficiently.

What do I need to bring to my first appointment?

When visiting our dental office for the first time, we recommend bringing a valid ID and your dental insurance information. This allows our team to verify coverage and review your dental history before treatment. If you have previous dental records or X-rays, bringing them can also be helpful. During your visit, our dentist will review your oral health and discuss possible treatment options. Our goal is to make your first appointment informative, comfortable, and helpful for your long-term dental health.
